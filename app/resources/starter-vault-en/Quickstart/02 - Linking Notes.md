# Linking Notes

What makes MindGraph special: You can **link notes** together — like Wikipedia, but for your own knowledge.

## How it works

1. Type `[[` in the editor
2. Pick a note from the list
3. Done! The link looks like this: [[Welcome]]

## Why link?

- Your knowledge becomes connected instead of hidden in folders
- You'll discover connections between topics
- **Backlinks** automatically show which notes link here

> **Tip:** Link related topics while writing — that's how you build your personal [[Knowledge Hub|knowledge network]].

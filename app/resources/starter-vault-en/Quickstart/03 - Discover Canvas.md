# Discover Canvas

The **Canvas** displays your linked notes as an interactive graph. See how your knowledge connects at a glance.

## Opening the Canvas

1. Click **Canvas** in the tab bar at the top
2. Or right-click a note and select "Open in Canvas"

## What you can do

- **Move notes** — drag and drop
- **Switch layouts** — Hierarchical, Grid, Smart
- **Zoom and navigate** — mouse wheel or trackpad
- **Toggle tags, links, and images** on or off

> **Try it:** Open the Canvas now and look at the [[Knowledge Hub]] — all notes in this vault are connected there!

# AI Features

MindGraph uses **local AI models** (Ollama or LM Studio). Your data never leaves your machine — no cloud, no costs, full privacy.

## What AI can do

### Quizzes & Flashcards
Right-click a note and select "Generate Quiz". The AI creates questions from your content. Perfect for [[Flashcards Example|exam preparation]].

### Chat with your notes
Open **Notes Chat** (toolbar, top right). Ask questions and the AI searches your notes for answers.

### Improve text
Select text, then right-click:
- **Summarize** — condense long texts
- **Translate** — into any language
- **Improve** — style and grammar

### Smart Connections
MindGraph finds semantically similar notes — even without explicit links. Enable it in Settings and Integrations.

## Setting up Ollama

1. Download [Ollama](https://ollama.com)
2. Install and start it
3. MindGraph detects Ollama automatically
4. Choose a model in Settings and Integrations

> **Recommended model:** Ministral 8B — good balance of quality and speed.

# Zotero Example

**Zotero** is a reference manager. MindGraph can directly access your Zotero library.

## Prerequisites

1. Install [Zotero](https://www.zotero.org/)
2. Install the [Better BibTeX](https://retorque.re/zotero-better-bibtex/) plugin
3. Start Zotero (runs in the background)

## Usage in MindGraph

- `Cmd+Shift+Z` opens the **Zotero Search**
- Search by title, author, or citekey
- Selected reference is inserted as a wikilink

## Example Reference

> "The limits of my language mean the limits of my world."
> - Ludwig Wittgenstein, *Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus* (1921)

## Why Zotero + MindGraph?

- Link literature directly in your notes
- Quotes and summaries next to your thoughts
- Everything stays local and under your control

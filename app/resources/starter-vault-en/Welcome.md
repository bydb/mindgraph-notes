# Welcome to MindGraph Notes!

Great to have you here. MindGraph Notes is your knowledge platform — connect notes, learn with local AI, everything on your machine.

Start with the [[01 - Getting Started|Quickstart]] or explore the [[Knowledge Hub|example notes]] right away.

## What makes MindGraph special?

- **Wikilinks**: Link notes with `[[` — your knowledge becomes a network
- **Canvas**: See your knowledge graph visually as a network
- **Local AI**: Generate quizzes, chat with notes, improve text — all private
- **Spaced Repetition**: Flashcards with a scientific learning algorithm

> All your notes are simple Markdown files. No lock-in, no subscription — your data belongs to you.
